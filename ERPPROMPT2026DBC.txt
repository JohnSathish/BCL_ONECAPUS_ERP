You are a senior enterprise software architect and full-stack ERP engineer.

Your task is to design and develop a modern, scalable, cloud-native College ERP system that fully supports India’s NEP-2020 (National Education Policy 2020) framework.

==================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
==================================================

Build a SaaS-based College ERP platform for:
- Colleges
- Universities
- Autonomous Institutions
- Multi-campus institutions

The system must support:
- CBCS (Choice Based Credit System)
- Academic Bank of Credits (ABC)
- Multidisciplinary programs
- Major/Minor degree structures
- Multiple entry/exit
- Skill-based courses
- Credit transfer
- Outcome-based education
- NAAC/NBA reporting
- AICTE/UGC compliance
- Role-based portals
- Multi-tenant architecture
